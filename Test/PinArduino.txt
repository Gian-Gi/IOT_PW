	 0 - RX - None
	 1 - TX - None
	 2 -    - INTERRUPT 1
	 3 - ~  - INTERRUPT 2
	 4 -    - Pin IN   : Arduino devices
	 5 - ~  - GreenLed : Arduino devices check OK
	 6 - ~  - Pin IN   : Raspberry ON
	 7 -    - RgbRed   :
	 8 -    - RgbGreen :
	 9 - ~  - RgbBlu   :
	10 - ~  - DS18B20  : Sensore digitale temperatura
	11 - ~  - 
	12 -    - 
	13 -    -
